Typically, your founder team meets with 3 of our Twip experts for 2 to 3 hours in a workshop format, to deepen our understanding of your scope and your technology: What makes you unique and why should we invest in you? If, at the end of the Pitch Workshop, we both decide to continue, the next step will be a Due Diligence, followed by our investment decision.
